Home: K-Lingo 101

Welcome to K-Lingo 101, a mini web-course created by a student from Guilford College. Here, you will find three simple introductory lessons on the Korean Language, starting with mastering the alphabet. Don’t worry if you’re uneasy about learning a new language. You will find this learning journey very rewarding and hopefully fun!

Here's What You Can Expect to Learn Throughout This Site:

  1. The Alphabet and Pronunciation
  2. Basic Reading & Writing skills
  3. Self-Introduction and Asking Questions
  4. Navigating Yourself and Basic Sentences
  5. Cool Cultural Facts!

K-Lingo 101 is created to be simple, fun, and easy to follow for any novice beginner. If you master the lessons in this website, you can basically navigate yourself in awesome places in South Korea, like the Namdaemun Market pictured above!

Without further ado, let's dive into the alphabet!
