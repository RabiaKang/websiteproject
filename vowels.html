The Alphabet: Vowels

Before getting into the 10 basic Korean vowels, let’s review some rules:

  1. Long versus Short English vowels: A (mate vs mat), E (bet vs seen), I (bit vs bite), O (dog vs home), U (bus vs mute).
  2. In Korean, there is no short “i” sound.
