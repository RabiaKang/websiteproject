Lesson 1 + Vocabulary

In this lesson, we will learn three grammar concepts that allow you to introduce yourself, say where you’re from and your job, and ask basic questions.

Before diving into grammar, first try getting familiar with these vocabulary sets! We will use these new words throughout this unit.

In Korean, to indicate your nationality, simply add “person” or “사람” after the country. For example, 미국 사람 indicates that you are American, though it literally means “USA person”.
